CHOICE RULES:
- Always include 3 choices related to the mission.
- Each choice must specify how it impacts the mission:
  - One "mission-advancing" choice (clear progress).
  - One "risky" choice (high risk, high reward or possible mission failure).
  - One "alternative" choice (indirect help, intel, new allies, or delay).
- Each choice MUST have a currency cost appropriate to its risk (e.g., $500 for low risk, $1000+ for high risk).
- Each choice MUST have a clear "consequence" to help the player decide.
- Each choice MUST include a "mission_impact" field explaining how it affects the mission.
- Label choices with "type": "mission-advancing", "risky", "alternative".

CUSTOM PLAYER CHOICE:
- The player will have an option to write their own approach (custom input).
- You DO NOT need to include this in the JSON. Just leave space in the narrative flow where it would make sense for a player to do something creative based on the situation.

Remember: The mission must feel like the central plot driver of this episode. Characters should react to it, and the story should revolve around the player's engagement with this mission.
